Kathleen McNamara


Born February 16th, 1993, Kathleen McNamara has always been an artist. After finding her love for performance art. She has taken on leadership rolls and performance oppotunities all along the east coast. Her specialty is fire and led performance art utilizing hoops, a dragon staff, a contact sword, and numerous other props.
